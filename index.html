Agent P

About Agent P

Agent P is a spy working for O.W.C.A., the government organisation of animal spies. His arch nemesis is called Doctor Doofenshmirtz. His first name is Perry.

Agent P in his spy costume

Agent P's Characteristics

  1. He's a greenish-teal platypus with yellow tangerine webbing on his back feet
  2. He has three dark heairs on his head
  3. His tail is long and salmon-orange coloured
  4. His eyes are dark brown, and are in an unnatural wall-eyed position, making him look mindlessly stupid
  5. He's known to wear a locket under his fur with a picture of him and Phineas and Ferb in it
  6. He wears a brown fedora (issued by O.W.C.A)

The very few times Agent P failed at his job

Agent P's job is to stop whatever evil sceme Dr. Doofenshmirtz is up to. Agent P (almost) always succeeds. Curse you Perry the Platypus!
